Altra Analytical Laboratories | 2022 – 2023

Role: Analytical Trainee

Conducted Quality Assurance and Quality Control testing for a Government Approved Public Testing Laboratory. This role provided rigorous training in GLP (Good Laboratory Practices) and precision instrumentation.

Key Skills & Responsibilities

  • Instrument Expertise: Gained hands-on expertise in High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC), Gas Chromatography (GC), FTIR, and UV spectrophotometry.
  • Calibration: Performed calibration of high-precision laboratory instruments to ensure data accuracy and compliance with regulatory standards.
  • Quality Control: Executed routine testing of pharmaceutical and chemical samples, ensuring adherence to pharmacopoeial standards.
